<idea>
**Design Movement:** Oxidized Precision / Editorial Dark Intelligence

**Core Principles:**
1. Instrument-grade restraint — every element earns its place, nothing decorative
2. Typographic hierarchy as the primary visual system
3. Dark field with warm oxidized accents — green-black, verdigris, bronze signal
4. Asymmetric column layouts that feel like a classified dossier

**Color Philosophy:**
Primary field: Instrument Black (#0A0F0D). The page breathes in near-darkness.
Accent: Signal (#C4A882) used once per screen — for CTAs and key callouts only.
Text hierarchy: Chart White (#E8EDE8) for headlines, Coordinate (#8A9E90) for body.
Surface variation: Deep Verdigris (#0D1A12) for cards and alternate sections.
Borders: Navigation (#1A3028) — barely visible structural skeleton.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Asymmetric editorial layout — left-weighted with generous right-side breathing room.
Section labels (IBM Plex Mono, spaced caps) float at the top-left of each section.
Content bleeds to the left edge; right side holds supporting text or white space.
No centered hero — the wordmark and headline align left, anchored like a navigation chart.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Navigation reticle SVG mark — constellation inside a sextant arc
2. Horizontal rule dividers with section numbers in monospace
3. Signal accent (#C4A882) used only on CTAs and one key statement per section

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Hover states reveal — content appears with subtle fade-in, not slide animations.
CTA button: solid Signal fill on hover, no border-radius (sharp corners only).
Scroll-triggered section labels fade in from opacity 0 — like instruments coming online.

**Animation:**
Entrance: sections fade in with a 200ms opacity transition, no vertical movement.
CTA hover: background fills from left to right in 150ms.
Section labels: stagger in 50ms apart on page load.

**Typography System:**
Display: IBM Plex Sans 200 — extreme lightness for large statements
Heading: IBM Plex Sans 300 — slightly heavier, still spare
Body: IBM Plex Sans 300 — readable, not heavy
Label: IBM Plex Mono 400 — spaced caps for section markers and metadata
Accent italic: IBM Plex Serif Italic — for contrast lines like "This one doesn't."

## Selected Direction

**OXIDIZED PRECISION** — Direction 1.

The brand guide explicitly recommends the Oxidized Signal palette and visual system. This direction honors that recommendation while building a landing page that feels like a classified intelligence document: dark, precise, asymmetric, and earned. The IBM Plex type system is already specified in the brand guide. The Signal accent (#C4A882) will be used sparingly — only on the primary CTA and one key statement per section. The navigation reticle logomark will anchor the hero.
